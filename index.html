Welcome to XR CareerAssist

Your virtual career coach, powered by the latest AI models, to guide you through your next steps.

Part of the EU funded VOXReality project.

About

XR-CareerAssist is transforming the way you plan and navigate your career through immersive Extended Reality (XR) experiences.

By integrating advanced AI models and analyzing over 100,000 real-world career paths, our platform delivers personalized, interactive guidance tailored to your unique skills and aspirations. With multilingual support and real-time feedback, XR-CareerAssist is accessible to users worldwide, ensuring you have the tools and confidence to take control of your professional journey. Dive into your future with a career assistant built for the modern world.

Our Vision

At XR-CareerAssist, our vision is to revolutionise career development by merging the power of AI and immersive XR technology. We aim to create a world where everyone has access to personalised, interactive career guidance—no matter their background or language. Our goal is to empower individuals to explore their potential, make informed decisions, and shape their professional futures with clarity and confidence. By bridging the gap between technology and human potential, we envision a future where career growth is engaging, accessible, and transformative for all.

Our Approach

At XR-CareerAssist, we combine cutting-edge technology with human-centric design to redefine career guidance. Our platform harnesses the power of AI models and a vast database of real-world career paths to deliver personalised, data-driven insights. Users engage through immersive XR environments, where they explore tailored career trajectories and interact with dynamic simulations and multilingual support, ensuring accessibility for all. By integrating real-time feedback and interactive learning, we continuously adapt and refine our tools to meet diverse user needs, making career development intuitive, engaging, and effective.
